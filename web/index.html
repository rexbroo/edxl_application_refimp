Welcome to the start page for composing your Emergency Data Exchange Language (EDXL) Message.

First choose the EDXL Message Type you need from among the message types shown in the panel to the right. Alternatively, you can start by composing EDXL-DE-v1.0 or EDXL-DE-v2.0 for your message

In subsequent pages you review, approve and send your message to its intended addressee(s)/recipient(s) through the EDXL Distribution Element (EDXL-DE) which wraps the electronic EDXL message payload like the envelope of a postal letter.
